How It Works

Technology Built for Real Cities

01

Real-Time Detection

ESP32-CAM units at every intersection. YOLO vision detects vehicle density and movement patterns in under 100ms.

02

AI Decision Engine

On-device inference — no cloud latency. Signal timing adjusts dynamically based on live congestion data, not schedules.

03
🚦

Signal Actuation

Commands sent to hardware in real time. Emergency corridors clear instantly. The system learns and improves every day.

04

Live Dashboard

City operators see heatmaps, wait times, fuel metrics, and incident alerts across all intersections in one view.

05

City Integration

Connects with existing municipal infrastructure via standard APIs. No ripping and replacing — intelligence layered on top.

06

Continuous Learning

Pattern data feeds back into model training — improving predictions for rush hours, events, and seasonal traffic shifts.
